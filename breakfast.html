Biscuits

Ingredients

2 cups sifted, all purpose flour
1 tbsp baking powder
1 tsp salt
6 tbsp shortening or bacon drippings
3/4 cup milk (fresh, sour, or buttermilk)

Preparation

Sift dry ingredients together
Cut in shortening
Stir in milk
Fold in flour to make soft dough
Knead the dough on lightly floured surface
Roll or pat out to about 1/2 inch thick
Cut and place on un-greased baking sheet
Bake at 450* for 10 - 12 minutes

Makes about 20 biscuits

French Toast

Ingredients

1 egg
1/2 cup sugar
1/4 to 1/2 tsp cinnamon
1 cup milk
bread

Preparation

Blend egg, sugar, and cinnamon
Add milk and mix
Dip sliced bread into mixture
Cook on a well-greased griddle, add a light coating of oil between batches to prevent sticking
Cook until sugar caramelizes and the toast turns brown
Butter toast and serve

Pancakes

Ingredients

2 eggs
2 1/2 cups milk
1/4 cup cooking oil
2 1/2 cups flour
1 tbsp baking powder
1 tsp salt

Preparation

Beat eggs, milk, and oil together in a large mixing bowl
Sift flour, baking powder, and salt together
Stir the dry ingredients into the liquid
Heat a well-oiled frying pan to 350*
Pour about 1/4 cup of batter for each cake onto the pan
Flip the cakes when bubbles come through to the top
