Cheese Straws

Ingredients

1 pound sharp cheddar cheese
3 1/2 cups sifted flower
1/4 tsp cayenne pepper
1/4 tsp salt
3/4 pound cold butter

Preparation

Sift flour, cayenne pepper, and salt together
Cut the ingredients together in a large mixing bowl
Keep the dough cold and in separate, easy-to-handle portions
Roll out on floured surface to about 1/4 inch thick
Cut into strips about 1 1/4 inches log
Spread apart on un-greased cookie sheet
Bake at 375* for 10 - 12 minutes

Makes about 10 1/2 dozen cheese straws. These treats are great for a party.
Make them ahead of tie and freeze the cut dough until you're ready to bake.

Dinner Rolls

Ingredients

3 packages of dry yeast
5 cups lukewarm water
1 cup sugar
2/3 cup shortening
2 tsp salt
4 pounds plain flour

Preparation

Mix yeast, sugar, and water in large bowl
Melt shortening and add yeast to mixture
Add salt
Slowly add flour
Knead into firm dough
Roll out and place in baking dish
Allow to set at room temperature for about two hours, or until rolls double in size
Bake at 375* for about 15 minutes

Yields 48 rolls
